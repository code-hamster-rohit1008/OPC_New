What is Own Professional Collaboration (OPC)?
OPC is a platform that connects creators, brands, and collaborators to build impactful partnerships that deliver measurable results.
Who can join OPC?
Creators, brands, and collaborators from various industries who value quality, creativity, and transparency.
Is there a cost to join OPC?
Joining is free, but certain premium services or campaign tools may involve charges.
How does OPC ensure a good match between partners?
We evaluate brand goals, creator style, and audience fit before recommending collaborations.
Do you work with international clients?
Yes, OPC works with both local and global partners.
Can I schedule a meeting with OPC?
Yes, you can schedule a meeting with our team directly through our website. Dates are subject to availability and any changes will be communicated.
What happens after I create my profile?
Your profile remains in pending verification until reviewed. Ensure accuracy of provided information to avoid delays.
Can I be disqualified from creating an account?
Yes, providing false or misleading information can result in immediate disqualification.
Who qualifies as a creator on OPC?
Any influencer, content creator, or creative professional with an engaged audience and quality content.
Do I need a minimum follower count to join?
No strict number — we focus on engagement, authenticity, and niche relevance.
How do I get brand deals through OPC?
Once verified, brands can connect with you directly or via curated campaigns.
Can I work with multiple brands at the same time?
Yes, unless bound by exclusivity agreements.
Will OPC help me improve my content?
Yes, OPC offers guidance and insights to align your work with brand expectations and market trends (in future).
Can I reject a brand offer?
Yes, you are free to accept or reject collaborations.
Do I need to share my personal phone number with brands?
No, all communications are handled through OPC’s secure messaging system.
What if a brand asks for work outside the agreed terms?
You can report it to OPC, and we will step in to mediate.
What types of brands does OPC work with?
We collaborate with startups, SMEs, and large companies across industries — fashion, tech, lifestyle, wellness, and more.
How do brands find the right creators?
We shortlist creators based on your campaign goals, target audience, and budget.
Do you handle campaign planning and execution?
Yes, from influencer selection to event planning and performance tracking.
How do I track my campaign’s success?
We provide measurable results, including reach, engagement, and conversion metrics.
Can I run multiple campaigns at once?
Yes, you can run several campaigns targeting different audiences simultaneously.
Do brands need verification before using OPC?
Yes, brands must sign up with an official company email and pass our verification process.
Can I work with international creators?
Yes, we connect brands with both domestic and international creators.
Can I request exclusive partnerships?
Yes, exclusivity options are available for certain campaigns.
Who can be a collaborator?
Photographers, event planners, stylists, videographers, PR professionals, or anyone adding value to brand-creator partnerships.
How do collaborators join campaigns?
Once registered, you can browse open collaboration opportunities and apply directly.
Can collaborators work with both creators and brands?
Yes, collaborators can offer services to either or both, depending on expertise.
Is payment handled through OPC?
Yes, we ensure secure transactions and timely payments.
Do I need prior industry experience to join as a collaborator?
No — talent, portfolio, and professionalism matter most.
Do collaborators need verification before joining?
Yes, collaborators must sign up using an official business email and complete verification.
Can I offer multiple services as a collaborator?
Yes, as long as they are relevant to campaign requirements.
Have more questions? Contact us and we’ll get back to you asap. It is just a small form to fill.
